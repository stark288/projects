What We Do

Health care is a service that includes medical services, manufacture medical equipment or drugs, provide medical insurance, or otherwise facilitate access to healthcare. Seeking a doctor for every single symptom of a disease is impossible. As part of reducing the time consuming process, we are developing a V Bot ( voice bot ) that helps customers and patients to identify symptoms and queries, and also provides Consulting services, Appointment scheduling, Voice recognition and Maps with nearby facilities. V Bot provides immediate access to customers or users from remote access to consult or search for any queries. Instantaneous results can be obtained through voice calls or chats.

Specialists & Doctors

Dentist

A Dentist is a healthcare professional who specializes in diagnosing, treating, and preventing oral health issues. They provide a wide range of services, including cleanings, fillings, extractions, and root canals. Regular visits to a dentist are essential for maintaining good oral health

Pediatrician

A pediatrician is a medical doctor who specializes in the care of infants, children, and adolescents. They diagnose and treat a wide range of medical conditions that affect children, including illnesses, injuries, and developmental issues.

Nutritionist

Nutritionists are experts in the field of nutrition who provide guidance on healthy eating habits, create personalized diet plans, and educate individuals on the importance of proper nutrition to support overall health and address specific dietary concerns or conditions.

Cardiologist

Cardiologists are specialized doctors who diagnose and treat conditions related to the heart and cardiovascular system, including heart disease, heart attacks, and high blood pressure, using various diagnostic tests and treatments to promote heart health and well-being.

Dermatologist

Dermatologists are medical professionals who diagnose and treat conditions related to the skin, hair, and nails, offering specialized care for issues like acne, eczema, psoriasis, and skin cancer through various treatments and procedures to maintain skin health and appearance.

Psychiatrist

Psychiatrists are doctors who specialize in mental health, diagnosing and treating mental illnesses such as depression, anxiety disorders, and schizophrenia using a combination of therapy, medication, and other interventions to support their patients' emotional and psychological well-being.

Services

Consulting Serivces

The appointment booking system is designed to streamline the process of scheduling appointments between patients and doctors in a medical establishment, such as a clinic or hospital. It provides an online platform where patients can request appointments with their chosen doctor, while also enabling doctors to manage their appointment schedules efficiently.

Emergency Services

The user benefits from the efficient access to nearby emergency services such as ambulance services, blood banks, and hospitals, made possible through location-based features.
